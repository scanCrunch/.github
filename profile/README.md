# 🍽️ scanCrunch

> **Scan. Browse. Order. Enjoy.**

ScanCrunch is a modern QR-based restaurant ordering platform that transforms the dining experience by enabling customers to scan a QR code, browse digital menus, place orders, make secure payments, and track their orders in real time—all without waiting for a waiter.

Designed for restaurants, cafés, hotels, and food courts, ScanCrunch streamlines operations while delivering a fast, contactless, and seamless customer experience.
